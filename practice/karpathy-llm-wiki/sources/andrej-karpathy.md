# Andrej Karpathy

Andrej Karpathy is a computer scientist and AI educator known for explaining deep learning and large language models clearly.

He worked at OpenAI and Tesla, and has been influential in helping engineers understand how modern neural networks are trained and used.

Common topics associated with Andrej Karpathy include:

- large language models
- tokenization
- transformer architectures
- training data pipelines
- neural network intuition
- practical AI engineering

He often emphasizes reducing repeated rediscovery by turning raw information into structured artifacts that can be reused over time.
